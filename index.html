About Me

I was born and raised in Provo, Utah by my amazing parents, Brian and Julie Holman. Their work ethic and love influenced me to work hard at my goals. I have a deep appreciation and love for my family members. They have made me who I am today. Through the influence of those around me, I eventually developed a love for tennis. It is an area in which you could say, I "thrive". I love to be outdoors especially in my hammock, and have recently developed a love for travelling. I am an Experience Design and Management student at Brigham Young University because I believe that people don't live their lives enough. There are so many opportunities that are missed because we are scared to do something new. My goal is to help people learn to live meaningful lives dedicated to things that will actually bring them fulfillment. Among these many factors, I believe service, faith, pursuit of passion, and experiences are what make a meaningful life.
